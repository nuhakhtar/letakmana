About Us 🌟

Letak Mana? is a fun educational game designed to help Malaysian children learn about tidiness and putting things where they belong.

🎯 How to play: Drag the object in the middle to the correct place. Only one zone is correct!

🏠 Our mission: Teaching kids early about being organized, tidy, and responsible through interactive play.

Made with love by Ihsan Anak for curious little minds!
