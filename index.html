About Us

Where Does It Go? is a fun educational game designed to help children learn about proper waste sorting.

How to play: Drag the object in the middle to the correct zone. Only one zone is correct!

Our mission: Teaching kids early about recycling, composting, and keeping our planet clean through interactive play.
